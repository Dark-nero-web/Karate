Heian Kata Series

🥋 Heian Shodan

The first kata, meaning 'Peaceful Mind 1'. Focuses on fundamental movements, Zenkutsu Dachi, and basic blocks.

🥋 Heian Nidan

The second kata. Introduces Kokutsu Dachi and more complex hand techniques including Nukite.

🥋 Heian Sandan

The third kata. Focuses on Kiba Dachi (Horse Stance) and close-range strikes and releases.
