GERI - Kicking Techniques

Range, Speed, and Devastating Power

Kicking Techniques

🦵 Mae Geri (Front Kick)

A basic but powerful snap kick delivered to the stomach or solar plexus using the ball of the foot.

🦵 Mawashi Geri (Roundhouse Kick)

A circular kick striking with the instep or shin. One of the most versatile kicks in karate.

🦵 Yoko Geri (Side Kick)

A thrusting or snapping kick delivered to the side using the edge of the foot (sokuto).
