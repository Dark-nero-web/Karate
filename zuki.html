ZUKI - Punching Techniques

Precision, Power, and Speed

Techniques Table

👊 Oi Zuki (Lunge Punch)

BEGINNER

A fundamental stepping punch where the arm and leg on the same side move forward together.

👊 Gyaku Zuki (Reverse Punch)

INTERMEDIATE

Executed with the arm opposite to the leading leg. This is the most powerful punch in Karate.

👊 Ren Zuki (Double Punch)

INTERMEDIATE

Two consecutive punches delivered at high speed.

👊 Kizami Zuki (Jab)

INTERMEDIATE

A quick leading hand punch intended to disrupt or score point.

👊 Yama Zuki (U-Punch)

ADVANCED

Simultaneous punches to the head and stomach forming a 'U' shape with the arms.
