Welcome to the Academy

Karate is a traditional martial art that originated in Okinawa and was later developed in mainland Japan. It emphasizes discipline, respect, and self-control, making it not only a physical activity but also a way of personal development.

BELT SYSTEM

⚪ White Belt - Beginner

The starting point for all karate students. White represents purity and a blank slate. Beginners learn fundamental stances, basic punching and kicking techniques.

🟡 Yellow Belt

The first colored belt representing the rising sun. Students develop better control over their techniques and learn the first Kata (Taikyoku Shodan).

🟠 Orange Belt

Representing growth and energy. Students at this level demonstrate improved form and technique consistency. They learn additional Kata and begin basic sparring.

🟢 Green Belt

Symbolizing growth and progress. Green belt students have developed solid fundamentals and begin learning intermediate Kata such as Heian Shodan.

🟣 Purple Belt

A transitional belt representing advancement towards higher levels. Students continue with Heian Kata series and improve their kumite skills.

🟤 Brown Belt

The final colored belt before black. Brown belt students have mastered many Kata and techniques, showing near black belt level performance.

⚫ Black Belt - DAN LEVELS

1st Dan (Shodan): Master level - demonstrates mastery of basics and advanced Kata. Each Dan requires years of dedicated training and mastery of karate philosophy.
