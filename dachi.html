DACHI - Strength of Foundation

Stability, Balance, and Power from the Ground

Fundamental Stances

🦶 Zenkutsu Dachi (Front Stance)

A long, low stance with 70% weight on the front leg. Provides maximum reach and forward power.

🦶 Kokutsu Dachi (Back Stance)

A defensive stance with 70% weight on the rear leg. Excellent for transitioning into counter-attacks.

🦶 Kiba Dachi (Horse Stance)

A wide, lateral stance with weight equally distributed. Develops strong legs and allows powerful side attacks.
