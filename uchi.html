Impact, Momentum, and Form

Striking Techniques

✋ Shuto Uchi (Knife Hand Strike)

Uses the broad part of the edge of the palm below the little finger. Effective for targeting the neck or collarbone.

👊 Uraken Uchi (Backfist Strike)

A snapping strike using the back of the first two knuckles. Known for its speed and unpredictability.

🤜 Empi Uchi (Elbow Strike)

Powerful close-range strike utilizing the elbow. Can be delivered in various directions (upward, downward, circular).
