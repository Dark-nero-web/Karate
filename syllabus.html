Belt Syllabus

Your Path to Mastery: Grading Requirements

⚪ 10th Kyu (White Belt)

Focus: Basics and Discipline

  • Kihon: Oi Zuki, Age Uke, Gedan Barai
  • Kata: Kihon Kata
  • Kumite: Gohon Kumite (Basics)

🟡 9th Kyu (Yellow Belt)

Focus: Stance and Balance

  • Kihon: Gyaku Zuki, Soto Uke, Mae Geri
  • Kata: Heian Shodan
  • Kumite: Gohon Kumite (Distance focus)

🟠 8th Kyu (Orange Belt)

Focus: Coordination and Flow

  • Kihon: Kokutsu Dachi, Shuto Uke, Mawashi Geri
  • Kata: Heian Nidan
  • Kumite: Sanbon Kumite

🟢 7th Kyu (Green Belt)

Focus: Power and Breath Control

  • Kihon: Yoko Geri Keage, Uraken Uchi
  • Kata: Heian Sandan
  • Kumite: Ippon Kumite

⚫ Shodan (Black Belt)

Focus: Mastery and Character

  • Kihon: All Advanced Combinations
  • Kata: Bassai Dai + Choice Kata
  • Kumite: Jiyu Kumite (Free Sparring)
