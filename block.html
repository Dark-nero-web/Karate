UKE - Blocking Techniques

Defense is the best Offense

Defensive Techniques

🛡️ Age Uke (Rising Block)

Deflects high-level attacks (e.g., to the head). The arm rises at an angle to force the attack upward and clear.

🛡️ Gedan Barai (Downward Block)

Used to defend against lower-level attacks like kicks. The arm sweeps across and down across the body.

🛡️ Soto Uke (Outside Block)

Moves from the outside inward to deflect middle-level attacks away from the center line.
